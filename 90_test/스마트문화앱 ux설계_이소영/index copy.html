About

French perfume brand. It is a popular Niche perfume brand. One of the earliest Niche perfumes introduced in Korea. Deeptyque means a two-tier folding screen in French. Although it is not Diptyque but Deep Tea, the majority of Koreans call it Diptyque, an unidentified pronunciation that is neither English nor Korean.
